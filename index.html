Introduction to EcoTrail

EcoTrack is an app that helps you track your carbon footprint. It lets you scan barcodes to see the environmental impact of products and suggests eco-friendly alternatives. The app also includes a chat forum where you can connect with others who care about the environment, share tips, and support each other in making greener choices.
