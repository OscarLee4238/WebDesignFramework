What is EcoTrail?

EcoTrack is an app that helps you track your carbon footprint. It lets you scan barcodes to see the environmental impact of products and suggests eco-friendly alternatives. The app also includes a chat forum where you can connect with others who care about the environment, share tips, and support each other in making greener choices.

Our goal?

Our objective is to create a digital tool designed to promote eco-friendly and socially responsible lifestyles We aim to simplify the process of reducing waste, adopting sustainable habits, and supporting ethical practices—whether through minimizing carbon footprints, responsible shopping, or living a greener lifestyle.
